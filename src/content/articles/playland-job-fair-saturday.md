Playland needs people for summer, and the pay isn't bad. The park is holding a job fair this Saturday, March 14, from 10 AM to 2 PM at the Westchester County Center (198 Central Avenue, White Plains). Parking is free.

They're hiring ride operators, ride attendants, lifeguards, cashiers, custodial staff, painters, and park management. Pay ranges from $17.10 to $20 per hour depending on the position. With the Dragon Coaster coming back online and a full season on the calendar through September, they need a full crew.

The first 150 people who applied online by March 11 were guaranteed interviews, but walk-ins are welcome. Bring a resume if you have one, dress like you care at least a little, and be ready to talk about your availability from late May through Labor Day.

This is the summer gig for Rye teens and college kids home for break. Lifeguard positions tend to go fast and usually require certification, so if you've got that, lead with it. Ride operator is the classic Playland job — long days, sun, and the ability to tell people to keep their hands inside the ride for three months straight.

**The details:**
- **When:** Saturday, March 14, 10 AM - 2 PM
- **Where:** Westchester County Center, 198 Central Ave, White Plains
- **Parking:** Free
- **Pay:** $17.10 - $20/hour
- **Positions:** Ride operators, lifeguards, cashiers, custodial, painters, management
