# Rye Garnets Hockey Heads to State Semifinals for First Time in 19 Years

## What happened / What's coming
The Rye Garnets ice hockey team beat Section 5 champion Aquinas 4-1 on Saturday, March 7 in Rochester to advance to the New York State semifinals. Goalie Fernando Mosquera made 50 saves on 51 shots. The Garnets (17-3-3) play in the state semifinals on Saturday, March 14 at LECOM Harborcenter in Buffalo. This is Rye's first trip to the state semis since 2007.

## Why it matters to Rye
This is the biggest Garnets hockey moment in nearly two decades. The team earned the state tournament's only at-large bid after losing to Byram Hills 3-2 in OT in the Section 1 finals, and now both teams are in the Final Four. The whole town will be watching Saturday.
