# Spring Arrives at Marshlands and Rye Nature Center — Guided Walks Coming Up

## What happened / What's coming
Marshlands Conservancy (220 Boston Post Road) is hosting a free guided spring walk on Saturday, March 28 from 10-11:30 AM to observe signs and patterns of nature in spring. The Rye Nature Center continues its weekend public programs and seasonal programming. The Rye Farmers Market returns May 3 on Theodore Fremd Avenue (Sundays, 9 AM - 2 PM).

## Why it matters to Rye
After a winter that saw temps swing from 68 degrees to snow within days (March 10 was 65F, March 12 forecast calls for rain/snow mix and highs of 51F dropping to 35F), spring is arriving unevenly. These outdoor programs are how Rye transitions into the season. Marshlands is one of Rye's greatest assets and the spring walk is a perfect low-key weekend outing.

## Key details
- Who: Marshlands Conservancy (Westchester County Parks), Friends of Rye Nature Center
- What: Guided spring nature walk; ongoing weekend programs
- When: March 28, 10-11:30 AM (Marshlands); Farmers Market opens May 3
- Where: 220 Boston Post Road (Marshlands); Theodore Fremd Ave (Farmers Market)
- How much: Free (Marshlands walk)
