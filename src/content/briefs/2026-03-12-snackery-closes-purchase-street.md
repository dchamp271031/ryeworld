# The Snackery Closes on Purchase Street After 4+ Years

## What happened / What's coming
The Snackery Bakeshop at 64 Purchase Street has closed. Owner Sara Leand announced the shutdown in January 2026, citing the loss of both parents and a need to refocus on family. The specialty bakery opened in October 2021 and gained national recognition, including an appearance on the Today show in January 2025. The Purchase Street storefront is now vacant.

## Why it matters to Rye
The Snackery was a beloved stop on Purchase Street — the kind of local bakery that becomes part of your routine. Its Today show appearance put Rye on the map nationally. The closure leaves a gap in downtown's food scene and opens up a prime retail spot at 64 Purchase Street. What fills that space will matter to everyone who walks the block.
