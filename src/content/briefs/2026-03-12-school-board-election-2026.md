# Two Rye School Board Seats Up for Election in May — Candidate Packets Available March 27

## What happened / What's coming
Two seats on the seven-member Rye City School District Board of Education are up for election on May 19, 2026. Incumbents Shaun Kloepfer and Tom Stein are eligible to run again. Candidate packets will be available starting March 27 at the District's Central Administration Offices (555 Theodore Fremd Avenue, Suite B-101). Petitions are due by April 29 at 5 PM.

## Why it matters to Rye
School board races in Rye are always consequential. The district consistently ranks among the best in Westchester, and board decisions on budgets, curriculum, and facilities affect every family. With candidate packets available in just two weeks, potential candidates need to start thinking now.

## Key details
- Who: Current board members Shaun Kloepfer and Tom Stein (seats up); Board President Jane Anderson, VP Matthew Schibanoff
- What: Two seats on the Board of Education
- When: Election May 19, 2026; packets available March 27; petitions due April 29 at 5 PM
- Where: 555 Theodore Fremd Avenue, Suite B-101 (packets); next Board meeting March 24 at 7:30 PM
