# Rye Real Estate: Median Prices Hit $2.3M as Spring Selling Season Heats Up

## What happened / What's coming
Rye's housing market remains a seller's market heading into spring 2026. Median listing prices are between $1.99M and $2.3M depending on the source, with multiple data points showing year-over-year increases. MyRye.com listed seven open houses for the weekend of March 7-8 alone. New luxury construction continues in the broader Rye/Rye Brook area with custom-built homes featuring 5+ bedrooms.

## Why it matters to Rye
Housing costs define who can live in Rye and shape the community's future. With the new comprehensive plan kicking off (housing pressures are explicitly cited as a reason for the update), real estate trends are directly tied to city policy. Spring is when the market accelerates and every open house on a Saturday becomes a neighborhood event.

## Key details
- Who: Rye homebuyers, sellers, and developers
- What: Seller's market with median prices near $2M+
- When: Spring 2026 selling season underway
- Where: City-wide, with notable activity in Hix Park, Milton Harbor, Mead Pond Estates areas
