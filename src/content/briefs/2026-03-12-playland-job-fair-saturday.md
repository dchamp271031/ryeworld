# Playland Job Fair This Saturday — Hiring Ride Operators, Lifeguards, Cashiers

## What happened / What's coming
Playland Park is hosting a job fair for its 2026 summer season this Saturday, March 14, from 10 AM to 2 PM at the Westchester County Center in White Plains. Open positions include park management, ride operators, ride attendants, lifeguards, cashiers, custodial, and painters. Pay ranges from $17.10 to $20/hour. The first 150 online applicants who submitted by March 11 were guaranteed interviews.

## Why it matters to Rye
Playland is Rye's amusement park and a major summer employer for local teens and young adults. With the Dragon Coaster returning and a full season planned, Playland needs a full crew. This is the time for Rye kids (and adults) looking for summer work.
