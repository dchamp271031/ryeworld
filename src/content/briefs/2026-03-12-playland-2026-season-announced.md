# Playland Announces 2026 Season: Dragon Coaster Returns, Fireworks Are Back

## What happened / What's coming
Westchester County released the full 2026 schedule and pricing for Playland's 98th season. The park opens for preseason on May 16-17, with grand opening on May 23. The Dragon Coaster, which was sidelined for much of 2025 after Standard Amusements exited its operating contract, will be back with replacement elements built from sturdier materials. Fourth of July and weekly Friday night fireworks are returning.

## Why it matters to Rye
Playland is Rye's backyard amusement park and a regional landmark. Last year's issues with Standard Amusements leaving and the Dragon Coaster being down were a big deal. The county stepping in and promising a strong 98th season is welcome news. Pricing staying relatively accessible matters to local families.

## Key details
- Who: Westchester County Parks, County Executive Ken Jenkins
- What: Playland 2026 season details and Dragon Coaster return
- When: Preseason May 16-17, Grand Opening May 23, runs through Sept 7 (Wed-Sun)
- Where: Playland Park, Rye
- How much: Parking $15/car ($20 holidays), admission FREE, wristbands $27 residents/$32 non-residents, season passes $85 residents/$100 non-residents
