# Rye City Council to Vote on $1.47M Fire Truck Purchase

## What happened / What's coming
The Rye City Council's March 11 agenda included a vote on purchasing a new $1.47 million Pierce pumper engine for the Rye Fire Department. The same meeting also covered awarding the 2026-2028 Rye Recreation Camp Transportation contract, a Stop DWI Patrol agreement with Westchester County, and a snow removal agreement. The next council meeting is March 25.

## Why it matters to Rye
A $1.5M fire truck is a significant capital expenditure for a city Rye's size. Fire equipment replacement cycles are long and the purchase signals investment in public safety infrastructure. The camp transportation contract affects every family that sends kids to Rye Rec programs.

- When: March 11, 2026 meeting (6:30 PM); next meeting March 25
